The group meets every Wednesday(1st session: September 7) from 2.30pm to 4.30pm at the Stadtteiltreff Höttinger Au. The aims of the group are simple:

  • to offer a wide range of toys, puzzles, books, sensory tunnels, songs etc to kids aged 0-6, whilst giving children the opportunity to interact and form new friendships in English.
  • to be supportive to Mummies, Daddies and Grandparents. The opportunity to speak to another adult(yay!) - and a chance to have a cup of coffee(donation based).
  • to embrace Circle Time and celebrate Anglophone festivals like Halloween, St Patrick’s Day, Holi etc

    • Note: While this group was originally created for families where English is one of the home languages, you don’t need to be English speakers to attend. Everyone is welcome.
